Conan

My Favorite Fictional Character

Conan Edogawa, the main character in the Detective Conan is a highly intelligent and skilled teenage detective named Shinichi Kudo. After being exposed to a mysterious poison, he is transformed into a child and takes on the pseudonym Conan Edogawa to hide his true identity. Despite his small stature, Conan continues to solve complex criminal cases while searching for a cure to return to his normal self. He possesses exceptional deductive abilities and a strong sense of justice, making him a captivating and enduring character in the series.

Reasons I admire Conan:

  • Intelligence and Deduction skills
  • Strong sense of justice
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Positive role model
  • Inspiration for learning
